Paramedic

Description

Paramedics provide emergency medical care and transport patients to medical facilities, often in life-threatening situations.

Salary Range

$40,000 - $70,000/year

Skills Required

Quick decision-making, emergency care, physical fitness, stress management.

Future Aspects

Essential role in emergency response systems; opportunities expanding globally.

Top Recruiters

EMS providers, hospitals, fire departments, government emergency services.

Famous Professionals

Kelly Grayson (EMS educator and advocate).
