Physiotherapist

Description

Physiotherapists help patients recover and improve physical function through exercises, therapy, and rehabilitation techniques following injuries or illnesses.

Salary Range

₹3,00,000 to ₹15,00,000 per annum depending on experience and specialization.

Skills Required

  • Knowledge of human anatomy and physiology
  • Interpersonal and communication skills
  • Patience and empathy
  • Physical stamina
  • Problem-solving skills

Future Aspects

Growing demand due to aging populations and awareness of physical health; opportunities in hospitals, sports clinics, rehabilitation centers, and private practice.
