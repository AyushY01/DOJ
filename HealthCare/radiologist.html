Radiologist

Description

Radiologists specialize in interpreting medical images such as X-rays, CT scans, and MRIs to diagnose illnesses and injuries.

Salary Range

₹8,00,000 to ₹35,00,000 per annum depending on experience and location.

Skills Required

  • Expertise in medical imaging techniques
  • Strong analytical skills
  • Attention to detail
  • Good communication with doctors and patients
  • Problem-solving abilities

Future Aspects

Advances in imaging technology and growing healthcare demands increase opportunities in hospitals, diagnostic centers, and research.

Top Recruiters

  • All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS)
  • Fortis Healthcare
  • Max Healthcare
  • Government Hospitals
  • Private Diagnostic Centers
