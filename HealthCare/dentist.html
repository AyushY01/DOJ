Dentist

Description

Dentists diagnose and treat dental issues, provide oral hygiene education, perform procedures like fillings, extractions, root canals, and maintain patients’ oral health.

Salary Range

₹4,00,000 to ₹20,00,000 per annum depending on experience and specialization.

Skills Required

  • Knowledge of dental anatomy and procedures
  • Manual dexterity and precision
  • Communication skills
  • Patient care and empathy
  • Problem-solving skills

Future Aspects

Expanding dental care awareness, new technologies, and cosmetic dentistry growth lead to rising demand; opportunities in private clinics, hospitals, and academic fields.
