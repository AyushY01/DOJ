Psychologist

Description

Psychologists study mental processes and behavior, helping individuals manage emotional and psychological issues.

Salary Range

$65,000 - $110,000/year

Skills Required

Empathy, analytical skills, patience, communication, research skills.

Future Aspects

Increasing demand in schools, hospitals, corporate wellness, and telehealth.

Top Recruiters

Government hospitals, mental health clinics, universities, MNCs, NGOs.

Famous Professionals

Dr. Albert Bandura (social cognitive theory), Dr. Carol Dweck (growth mindset).
