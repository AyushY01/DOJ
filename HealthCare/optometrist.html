Optometrist

Description

Optometrists diagnose vision problems and prescribe corrective lenses and treatments.

Salary Range

$70,000 - $120,000/year

Skills Required

Clinical knowledge, patient care, attention to detail, communication.

Future Aspects

Increasing demand due to aging population and screen-related vision issues.

Top Recruiters

Lenskart, Vision Express, Specsavers, Apollo Hospitals, AIIMS.

Famous Professionals

Dr. A. Halberstam (noted optometric educator).
