Nurse

Description

Nurses provide patient care, assist doctors, administer medications, monitor patient health, and support recovery in hospitals, clinics, and community settings.

Salary Range

₹2,50,000 to ₹8,00,000 per annum depending on qualifications and experience.

Skills Required

  • Compassion and empathy
  • Attention to detail
  • Communication skills
  • Physical stamina
  • Ability to work under pressure

Future Aspects

Growing healthcare needs increase demand for skilled nurses, with opportunities in specialized nursing, management, and education.
