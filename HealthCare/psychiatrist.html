Psychiatrist

Description

Psychiatrists are medical doctors who diagnose, treat, and help prevent mental, emotional, and behavioral disorders.

Salary Range

$90,000 - $200,000/year

Skills Required

Clinical knowledge, emotional resilience, diagnostic skills, communication.

Future Aspects

Growing due to rising mental health awareness and demand for psychiatric care.

Top Recruiters

Government and private hospitals, mental health institutions, private practice.

Famous Professionals

Dr. Sigmund Freud (founder of psychoanalysis), Dr. Aaron Beck (cognitive therapy).
