Pharmacist

Description

Pharmacists prepare and dispense medications, advise patients on drug use, and ensure the safe and effective use of pharmaceutical drugs.

Salary Range

₹3,00,000 to ₹12,00,000 per annum depending on experience and workplace.

Skills Required

  • Knowledge of drugs and pharmacology
  • Attention to detail
  • Communication skills
  • Analytical and problem-solving ability
  • Ethics and responsibility

Future Aspects

Increasing demand in healthcare and pharmaceutical industries with opportunities in hospitals, retail pharmacies, research, and drug manufacturing.
