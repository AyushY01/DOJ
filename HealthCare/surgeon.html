Surgeon

Description

Surgeons perform operations to treat injuries, diseases, deformities, and improve body function. They specialize in various surgical fields such as general, orthopedic, cardiovascular, and neurosurgery.

Salary Range

₹10,00,000 to ₹50,00,000 per annum depending on specialization and experience.

Skills Required

  • Manual dexterity and precision
  • Strong knowledge of human anatomy
  • Decision-making under pressure
  • Team coordination and communication
  • Patience and stamina

Future Aspects

High demand for specialized surgeons with increasing healthcare complexity and advanced medical technologies. Opportunities in hospitals, research, and private practice.
