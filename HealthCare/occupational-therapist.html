Occupational Therapist

Description

Helps people regain daily life skills after illness, injury, or disability.

Salary Range

$60,000 - $100,000/year

Skills Required

Creativity, communication, problem-solving, patience.

Future Aspects

Demand rising in elder care, mental health, and rehabilitation.

Top Recruiters

Rehabilitation centers, hospitals, old-age homes, NGOs.

Famous Professionals

Dr. Anne Cronin Mosey (contributor to OT theory).
