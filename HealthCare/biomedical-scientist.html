Biomedical Scientist

Description

Biomedical scientists perform laboratory tests on specimens to help diagnose and treat diseases.

Salary Range

$50,000 - $90,000/year

Skills Required

Analytical thinking, lab techniques, attention to detail, teamwork.

Future Aspects

Growing due to innovations in diagnostics, biotechnology, and public health.

Top Recruiters

PathLabs, SRL Diagnostics, NHS, Apollo Hospitals, Quest Diagnostics.

Famous Professionals

Sir Alec Jeffreys (developed DNA fingerprinting).
