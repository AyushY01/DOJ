Doctor (Physician)

Description:

Doctors are responsible for diagnosing illnesses, prescribing medications, and providing comprehensive treatment plans. They work across various specialties, such as general medicine, pediatrics, cardiology, and more.

Salary Range:

₹10,00,000 – ₹35,00,000 per year in India; can vary based on specialty and experience.

Skills Required:
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Strong diagnostic and problem-solving ability
  • Empathy and patient-centered care
  • Deep knowledge of human biology and diseases
Future Aspects:

Medical professionals continue to be in high demand due to aging populations and emerging health challenges. Opportunities in telemedicine, research, and healthcare administration are growing.

Top Recruiters:
  • Apollo Hospitals
  • Fortis Healthcare
  • AIIMS
  • Medanta
  • Government Health Departments
  • Private clinics and startups (e.g., Practo, 1mg)
