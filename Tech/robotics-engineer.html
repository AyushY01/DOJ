Robotics Engineer

Description

Robotics Engineers design, build, program, and test robots and automated systems to perform tasks in manufacturing, healthcare, and other industries.

Salary Range (India)

  • Entry-Level: ₹4 – ₹8 LPA
  • Mid-Level: ₹8 – ₹15 LPA
  • Senior-Level: ₹15 – ₹30+ LPA

Skills Required

  • Knowledge of robotics programming languages (C++, Python, ROS)
  • Understanding of mechanical design and electronics
  • Experience with sensors, actuators, and control systems
  • Problem-solving and analytical thinking
  • Team collaboration and project management

Future Aspects

Robotics is rapidly expanding in sectors like manufacturing, healthcare, logistics, and agriculture, with AI integration increasing the complexity and capability of robots.

Top to Mid-Tier Companies That Recruit Robotics Engineers

  • Top-Tier: ABB, Boston Dynamics, Fanuc, KUKA Robotics
  • Mid-Tier: GreyOrange, Hi-Tech Robotics, Tata Elxsi
  • Others: Startups, research institutes, automotive manufacturers

Famous Robotics Engineers

  • Rodney Brooks – Pioneer in robotics and co-founder of iRobot
  • Marc Raibert – Founder of Boston Dynamics
  • Hiroshi Ishiguro – Known for humanoid robots
