Structural Engineer

Description

Structural Engineers analyze, design, and oversee the construction of buildings, bridges, tunnels, and other structures to ensure safety and durability under loads and environmental conditions.

Salary Range (India)

  • Entry-Level: ₹3 – ₹6 LPA
  • Mid-Level: ₹6 – ₹12 LPA
  • Senior-Level: ₹12 – ₹25+ LPA

Skills Required

  • Expertise in structural analysis software (STAAD.Pro, ETABS, SAP2000)
  • Knowledge of materials science and load calculations
  • Ability to interpret blueprints and technical drawings
  • Strong problem-solving and mathematical skills
  • Project management and communication skills

Future Aspects

Growing urban infrastructure and focus on earthquake-resistant and sustainable structures will increase demand. Integration of AI and BIM technologies offers modern tools for structural engineers.

Top to Mid-Tier Companies That Recruit Structural Engineers

  • Top-Tier: L&T Construction, Shapoorji Pallonji, AECOM, Jacobs Engineering
  • Mid-Tier: Buro Happold, COWI, Arcadis
  • Others: Government agencies, infrastructure firms, consulting companies

Famous Structural Engineers

  • Fazlur Rahman Khan – Father of tubular designs in skyscrapers
  • Emily Roebling – Key engineer behind Brooklyn Bridge
  • Othmar Ammann – Designer of many NYC bridges
