Nuclear Engineer

Description

Nuclear Engineers research and develop processes, instruments, and systems to harness nuclear energy and radiation safely and efficiently. They work on nuclear power plants, medical imaging, and radiation safety.

Salary Range (India)

  • Entry-Level: ₹5 – ₹9 LPA
  • Mid-Level: ₹9 – ₹18 LPA
  • Senior-Level: ₹18 – ₹35+ LPA

Skills Required

  • Reactor physics and nuclear fuel cycle
  • Radiation protection and safety protocols
  • Thermal hydraulics and reactor design
  • Regulatory compliance and quality assurance
  • Strong analytical and computational skills

Future Aspects

Growing interest in clean energy, nuclear medicine, and advanced reactors drives demand for nuclear engineers, especially in sustainable energy initiatives.

Top to Mid-Tier Companies That Recruit Nuclear Engineers

  • Top-Tier: Bhabha Atomic Research Centre (BARC), Nuclear Power Corporation of India Limited (NPCIL), Westinghouse Electric
  • Mid-Tier: Larsen & Toubro (L&T), Areva, Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO)
  • Others: Defense R&D organizations, nuclear medicine companies

Famous Nuclear Engineers

  • Enrico Fermi – Architect of the first nuclear reactor
  • Lise Meitner – Contributor to nuclear fission discovery
