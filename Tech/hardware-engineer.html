Hardware Engineer

Description

Hardware Engineers design, develop, and test physical components of computers, embedded systems, and electronic devices.

Salary Range (India)

  • Entry-Level: ₹3 – ₹6 LPA
  • Mid-Level: ₹6 – ₹12 LPA
  • Senior-Level: ₹12 – ₹25+ LPA

Skills Required

  • Strong understanding of digital and analog circuits
  • Knowledge of microprocessors, FPGA, and PCB design
  • Experience with hardware description languages (HDL) like VHDL/Verilog
  • Familiarity with testing and debugging tools
  • Ability to collaborate with software and firmware teams

Future Aspects

With the growth of IoT, wearables, and embedded systems, demand for hardware engineers is increasing globally.

Top to Mid-Tier Companies That Recruit Hardware Engineers

  • Top-Tier: Intel, Qualcomm, Texas Instruments, Broadcom
  • Mid-Tier: Cypress Semiconductor, Microchip Technology, Tata Elxsi
  • Others: Startups, defense sector companies

Famous Hardware Engineers

  • Robert Noyce – Co-inventor of the integrated circuit
  • Gordon Moore – Co-founder of Intel and author of Moore's Law
  • Steve Wozniak – Co-founder of Apple and hardware innovator
