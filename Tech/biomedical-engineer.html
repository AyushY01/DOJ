Biomedical Engineer

Description

Biomedical Engineers design and develop medical devices and technologies to improve healthcare delivery and patient outcomes.

Salary Range (India)

  • Entry-Level: ₹3 – ₹6 LPA
  • Mid-Level: ₹6 – ₹12 LPA
  • Senior-Level: ₹12 – ₹25+ LPA

Skills Required

  • Knowledge of biology, medical sciences, and engineering principles
  • Experience with medical device design and regulatory standards
  • Proficiency in CAD and simulation software
  • Data analysis and problem-solving skills
  • Interdisciplinary teamwork and communication

Future Aspects

The rising demand for advanced medical technologies and aging populations worldwide create strong growth prospects for biomedical engineers.

Top to Mid-Tier Companies That Recruit Biomedical Engineers

  • Top-Tier: Medtronic, GE Healthcare, Philips Healthcare
  • Mid-Tier: Siemens Healthineers, Wipro GE Healthcare
  • Others: Hospitals, research institutions, medical device startups

Famous Biomedical Engineers

  • Robert Langer – Pioneer in biomedical engineering and drug delivery systems
  • Florence Nightingale – Founder of modern nursing and healthcare statistics
