Chemical Engineer

Description

Chemical Engineers design and operate processes that convert raw materials into valuable products, such as fuels, chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and food. They ensure safety, efficiency, and sustainability in chemical manufacturing.

Salary Range (India)

  • Entry-Level: ₹3 – ₹7 LPA
  • Mid-Level: ₹7 – ₹14 LPA
  • Senior-Level: ₹14 – ₹30+ LPA

Skills Required

  • Process design and simulation (Aspen Plus, HYSYS)
  • Chemical reaction engineering
  • Heat and mass transfer
  • Safety and environmental regulations
  • Problem-solving and analytical skills

Future Aspects

Growing demand for sustainable energy, green chemistry, and pharmaceutical manufacturing drives career growth for chemical engineers worldwide.

Top to Mid-Tier Companies That Recruit Chemical Engineers

  • Top-Tier: Reliance Industries, BASF, Linde India, Tata Chemicals
  • Mid-Tier: Indian Oil Corporation, Honeywell, Aker Solutions
  • Others: Pharma companies, specialty chemicals firms

Famous Chemical Engineers

  • George E. Davis – Founder of chemical engineering as a discipline
  • Robert Langer – Pioneer in drug delivery and biotechnology
